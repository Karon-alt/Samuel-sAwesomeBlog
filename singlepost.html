Here are 12 awesome facts about sharks!

  1. Sharks have been around for 450 million years!
  2. Shark skin feels similar to sandpaper.
  3. Scientists age sharks by counting the rings on their vertebrae.
  4. Sharks do not have bones.
  5. The whale shark is the largest fish in the ocean.
  6. Most sharks have good eyesight.
  7. Sharks can go into a trance when flipped over.
  8. Blue sharks are really blue.
  9. Different shark species reproduce in different ways.
  10. An estimated 100 million sharks are killed every year.
  11. There are over 500 species of shark.
  12. Hammerhead sharks sometimes like to swim in schools.s

Sharks are at the top of the food chain. They are so cool and I hope I get to swim with sharks one day!
